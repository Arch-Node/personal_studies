
# 📡 Communication & Digital Security  

## 📜 Quick Summary  
✅ **Use encrypted communication tools to avoid surveillance.**  
✅ **Never rely on centralized platforms like Facebook for organizing.**  
✅ **Use offline networks (Briar, USB dead drops) when needed.**  
✅ **Regularly check for spyware & secure personal devices.**  

## 1️⃣ Secure Messaging & Communication  
| Tool | Encryption | Works Offline? | Best Use Case |
|------|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| **Signal** | ✅ Yes | ❌ No | Secure messaging |
| **Briar** | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes | Offline protest coordination |
| **Session** | ✅ Yes | ❌ No | Anonymous communication |

## 2️⃣ Secure Social Media Usage  
- **Create burner accounts** – Never link real-world identity.  
- **Use VPNs & Tor** – Prevent IP tracking.  
- **Mirror critical data across multiple platforms** – Reduces risk of takedowns.  

## 3️⃣ Secure Offline Communication  
| Method | Security Level | Best Use Case |
|--------|--------------|---------------|
| **USB Dead Drops** | ✅ High | Sharing sensitive files offline |
| **Radio & Encrypted QR Codes** | ✅ High | Alternative to internet-based distribution |
| **One-Time Pads** | ✅ Very High | Military-grade secret messaging |
